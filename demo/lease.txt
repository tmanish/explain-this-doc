RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is entered into on August 1, 2026 between Bluestone
Property Management LLC ("Landlord") and Jordan Avery ("Tenant") for the
premises located at 412 Maple Grove Lane, Apartment 3B.

1. TERM. The lease term begins on September 1, 2026 and ends on
August 31, 2027. This lease will automatically renew for successive
twelve-month terms unless either party gives written notice at least
sixty (60) days before the end of the current term.

2. RENT. Tenant shall pay monthly rent of $1,850.00, due on the first
day of each month. A late fee of $75.00 applies to any payment received
after the fifth day of the month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $2,775.00
prior to move-in. A cleaning fee of $250.00 is non-refundable and will
be deducted from the deposit at move-out.

4. EARLY TERMINATION. If Tenant terminates the lease early, Tenant shall
pay an early termination fee equal to two months' rent and forfeit the
security deposit.

5. PETS. No pets are permitted without prior written approval. An
approved pet requires a pet deposit of $500.00 and pet rent of $50.00
per month.

6. MAINTENANCE. Tenant is responsible for all repairs under $200.00,
including plumbing clogs, light fixtures, and appliance maintenance.
Landlord shall not be liable for damage to Tenant's personal property.

7. GUESTS. Guests staying more than seven (7) consecutive days require
Landlord approval.

8. UTILITIES. Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, internet, and
water. Landlord covers trash collection.

9. DISPUTES. Any dispute arising under this lease shall be resolved by
binding arbitration. Tenant hereby waives the right to a jury trial.

10. RENEWAL NOTICE. Rent for any renewal term may increase by up to 8%
with thirty (30) days written notice.

Signed,
Bluestone Property Management LLC
