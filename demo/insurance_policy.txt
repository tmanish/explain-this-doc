HARBORLIGHT INSURANCE COMPANY
Homeowners Policy Declarations and Summary

Policyholder: Jordan Avery
Policy Period: October 1, 2026 to October 1, 2027

PREMIUM AND DEDUCTIBLES
Annual Premium: $2,340.00, payable annually or in monthly installments
of $199.00 (installment plan includes a $6.00 monthly service fee).
Standard Deductible: $2,500.00 per claim.
Wind and Hail Deductible: 2% of dwelling coverage per claim.

COVERAGE LIMITS
Dwelling: $385,000.00
Personal Property: $192,500.00
Personal Liability: $300,000.00
Loss of Use: $77,000.00

EXCLUSIONS
This policy does not cover flood damage, earthquake damage, sewer
backup (unless endorsement purchased), damage from neglect or lack of
maintenance, or business activities conducted on the premises.

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
Claims must be reported within sixty (60) days of the loss. The insured
must take reasonable steps to prevent further damage and must provide a
proof of loss statement within ninety (90) days if requested.

RENEWAL AND CANCELLATION
This policy will automatically renew at the end of the policy period at
then-current rates unless cancelled. The company may cancel with thirty
(30) days notice. Premium refunds for mid-term cancellation are subject
to a short-rate penalty of up to 10%.
